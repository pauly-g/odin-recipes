Pauly's Famous Ham and Cheese Crepe Recipe

Saturday Morning Savoury Crepe

Your family will beg you to make these crepes every weekend once they taste them!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 3 table spoons of butter
  • 3 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of Vanilla essence
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Method

  1. Mix all of the ingredients in the blender/li>
  2. Chill in the firdge for 20+ minutes to 24 hours
  3. Pour mixture into well buttered fry pan
  4. Add cheese and ham and then fold crepe in half after 2 minutes on medium heat
  5. Cook for a further 1 minute then serve
  6. Add butter before you pour each new crepe
